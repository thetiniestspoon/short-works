---
title: Transcendent Love
type: poetry
---

Dear Lord! the world is spinning in well-versed pirouettes
I toss in my sleep at night, music reeling in my head
Prima on trumpet!
That tenacious tongue tickling and trotting a tune
as I dream of the night when I met hir.

Heaven was beside me as we traipsed hand in hand
Out around town, our heads and hearts
half-tipsy on gypsy wine
In hir eyes I could see the stars sighing...
and impressing upon me the most divine frustrations.

Oh Lord! once I would have stretched away from hir fingertips
but when they danced along my skin
I could only sway into hir arms
and let go of that faded thing I used to be.

The sky splintered into streams of light
that zipped by and ripped open the night
The treetops mumbled and whispered as the quickening storm drummed and thumped
heavy handfuls of rain upon us.

"It's going to really storm," I whispered
and my stomach turned at the dreadful thought of this dream waning
My Lord!

Ze pulled me closer and brought our hearts together and replied
"Let it."

As we kissed and embraced amidst the furious storm
I could have sworn that over the roar
I heard the sighs and soars of an angel trumpeting
to the quickening step of the Earth dancing round its axis.
