# Parallax Scene Image Prompts

Generate each image at **21:9 aspect ratio** (ultrawide panoramic). Style: **moody, atmospheric, painterly** with muted/desaturated warm tones. Each scene has 3 layers — generate each as a separate image.

For layers marked **(transparent)**: generate the element on a plain dark background, then remove the background in post to get a PNG with alpha transparency. The background layer does NOT need transparency.

## Scene 1: Brooklyn Summer
*After "Brooklyn summer" — romantic, dusky, urban*

**Background (brooklyn-bg.png):**
Panoramic dusk skyline of lower Manhattan seen from across the East River. Deep indigo sky bleeding into rose and amber at the horizon. City lights just beginning to flicker on. Neon reflections in calm water. Painterly, slightly impressionistic. Warm muted palette — no harsh colors.

**Midground (brooklyn-mid.png) — transparent:**
Old Brooklyn waterfront — rusted warehouse silhouettes, graffiti-streaked fences, cobblestone street edge. Seen from pier level. Dark shapes against the sunset glow. Industrial romance.

**Foreground (brooklyn-fg.png) — transparent:**
Wooden pier railing in close-up, slightly out of focus. Iron bollards. The railing frames the view like a theatre proscenium. Dark, nearly silhouetted, with just enough detail to read as weathered wood and iron.

## Scene 2: Pancakes
*After "Pancakes" — warm, nostalgic, morning*

**Background (pancakes-bg.png):**
Soft golden morning light streaming through a kitchen window. Warm amber and honey tones. The light itself is the subject — rays catching dust motes, casting long warm shadows. Slightly overexposed, dreamy quality. Think Vermeer's light but in a 1990s kitchen.

**Midground (pancakes-mid.png) — transparent:**
A cast iron skillet seen from above, with a single golden pancake. Steam wisps rising. Butter melting. The warmth is palpable. Painterly, slightly abstracted — not photorealistic.

**Foreground (pancakes-fg.png) — transparent:**
Close-up: syrup bottle edge, a fork, the curve of a plate rim. Domestic still life fragments, slightly blurred. Dark maple-amber tones.

## Generation Tips

- For **transparent layers**: generate on a solid dark background, then use the "remove background" feature in your image tool, or generate with explicit "on transparent background" / "isolated element, no background" in the prompt
- For **background layers**: no transparency needed — these are full-bleed atmospheric images
- Target resolution: **2100x900px** minimum (21:9 at decent quality)
- Keep file sizes reasonable — optimize PNGs (background layers can be JPEG instead)
- Test: drop even one scene's 3 images in and rebuild to see the effect before doing all 30
